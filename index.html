Impacket Cheatsheet

Kali Linux format — impacket-[tool] | all examples use DOMAIN/user:Pass@target
Auth flags (work on most tools):
Password: DOMAIN/user:Pass@target  |  Pass-the-Hash: -hashes :NTHASH  |  Kerberos: -k -no-pass (set KRB5CCNAME first)  |  AES key: -aesKey HEX  |  DC: -dc-ip 192.168.1.1
